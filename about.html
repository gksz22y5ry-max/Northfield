About

Dewson Websites is built on clarity, trust, and solid delivery.

We design, build, and maintain professional websites for small UK businesses. If you don’t have a website yet, or yours feels outdated and ineffective, we’ll help you fix that with clear steps and clear pricing.

Who we are

Dewson Websites is co-owned by Edward Dewhurst and Jake Dickinson. Edward leads technical oversight, ensuring builds are robust, fast, and maintainable. Jake leads commercial and sales oversight, keeping the process simple, transparent, and client-friendly.

We keep the emphasis on clarity and trust: plain-English explanations, fixed-price packages, optional upgrades, and a 60-day post-launch warranty to reduce your risk.

What we value

  • Minimal, elegant design
  • Fast, accessible builds
  • Clear communication
  • Long-term partnership
